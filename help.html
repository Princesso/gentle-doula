Gentle Doula

Support resources

If you feel unsafe, overwhelmed, or notice persistent sadness, please reach out to a professional. This app does not replace medical care.

Immediate help

  • Call your local emergency number if you are in danger.
  • Contact your midwife, OB, or GP for urgent concerns.
  • Postpartum International: postpartum.net (country-specific helplines)

Helpful steps

  • Share how you feel with a trusted person.
  • Plan small breaks, hydration, and light movement.
  • Use the app to log notes and track patterns to discuss with your clinician.
